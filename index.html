Student Government (WIP)

I served on the Student Government Council at Florida State University Panama City Campus for 5 semesters.

I first served as the Historian, my main job was to photograph events and representatives. While in this position I took on the responsibilities of the Publicity chair also and eventually contributed to the merging of those two positions.

The second semester I served as the Programming chair. That position consisted of organizing by-weekly events for the students on campus

After Programming I became Finance chair where I balanced the Student Government budget and audited timecards for accuracy

Finally I was appointed as the President Pro Tempore, or Vice President. This is the position I held most recently and the responsibilities consist of being parlimentarian, disciplinarian, and right-hand man to the President.
